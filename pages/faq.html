Questions

Most common questions about backing, shipping, PnP files, refunds, and using the TabletopForge backer portal.

Still need help? Backer Portal

Shipping & Fulfillment

How do I track my order?

Log into your Backer Portal and visit the "Shipping Tracker" section. You'll see real-time updates from factory production through final delivery.

Timeline:

  • Order confirmed → Production → Factory freight → Fulfillment hub → Local delivery

EU/US orders typically 4–6 weeks from campaign end. International 6–10 weeks.

When will my game ship?

Shipping dates are posted in your dashboard and campaign updates. Current timelines:

  • Citadels: Q4 2026 (live campaign)
  • Verdant: Apr 1–5 (89% shipped)
  • Iron Pact: Delivered Feb 2024

Delays happen. We communicate transparently via forum + email.

What shipping carriers do you use?

Partners: Royal Mail (UK), DHL/USPS (EU/US), FedEx/UPS (international). Tracked service standard.

Costs: Included in pledge. No surprise fees.

Print & Play Files

Where do I download PnP files?

Backer Portal → "Print & Play" section. Files available immediately after campaign ends.

Formats: Full colour PDF + printer-friendly B&W. Print at home or copy shop.

Do I need special materials for PnP?

Standard home printer + cardstock/paper. Optional:

  • Chipboard (1mm) for tokens
  • Laminator sheets
  • Board game punchboard

Full instructions + templates included in every file.

Backing & Refunds

Can I change my pledge?

Yes, until campaign closes. Visit the live Kickstarter/Gamefound page → "Manage Pledge".

After campaign: Contact us via dashboard → Support. Limited options post-production.

What is your refund policy?

Campaign: Kickstarter policy applies (pledges non-refundable after successful funding).

Post-campaign:

  • Before production: full refund
  • After production: 50% refund or replacement
  • Delivered + defective: full refund/replacement

Backer Portal

How do I log into the portal?

Use the same email + password from Kickstarter/Gamefound. Click here to login.

Forgot password? Use the forgot password link on the login page.

I backed on Kickstarter — how do I connect?

Pledge email auto-imports. If not showing, contact support with your KS email address.

Community & Forum

Who can post on the forum?

Verified TabletopForge backers only. Forum access in your backer portal.

Community guidelines: read before posting.

Can't find your answer?

Reach out to our support team. Response within 24 hours.
