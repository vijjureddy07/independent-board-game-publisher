Shipping & Fulfillment

How do I track my order?

Log into your Designer Studio and visit the "Shipping Tracker" section. You'll see real-time updates from factory production through final delivery.

Timeline:

  • Order confirmed → Production → Factory freight → Fulfillment hub → Local delivery

EU/US orders typically 4–6 weeks from order date. International 6–10 weeks.

When will my game ship?

Shipping dates are posted in your designer studio dashboard. Current timelines:

  • Citadels: Q4 2026 (in production)
  • Verdant: Apr 1–5 (89% shipped)
  • Iron Pact: Delivered Feb 2024

Delays happen. We communicate transparently via forum + email.

What shipping carriers do you use?

Partners: Royal Mail (UK), DHL/USPS (EU/US), FedEx/UPS (international). Tracked service standard.

Costs: Included in order total. No surprise fees.

Print & Play Files

Where do I download PnP files?

Designer Studio → "Print & Play" section. Files available immediately after your order is confirmed.

Formats: Full colour PDF + printer-friendly B&W. Print at home or copy shop.

Do I need special materials for PnP?

Standard home printer + cardstock/paper. Optional:

  • Chipboard (1mm) for tokens
  • Laminator sheets
  • Board game punchboard

Full instructions + templates included in every file.

Do PnP files get updated after feedback?

Yes. Rules fixes, balance updates, and print optimizations are pushed to the same file library whenever a revision ships.

Your dashboard always shows the latest version number, and older files are archived for reference.

Orders & Refunds

Can I change my order?

Yes, before production begins. Contact us via dashboard → Support to modify your order.

After production starts: Contact us via dashboard → Support. Limited options post-production.

What is your refund policy?

Orders: Once in production, orders are non-refundable (see below for exceptions).

Details:

  • Before production: full refund
  • After production: 50% refund or replacement
  • Delivered + defective: full refund/replacement
Can I update my address after ordering?

Yes, up until address lock. Use the designer studio to update delivery details before production closes.

After address lock, contact support immediately and we will confirm whether your parcel can still be rerouted.

Designer Studio

How do I log into the Designer Studio?

Use your registered email and password. Click here to login.

Forgot password? Use the forgot password link on the login page.

How do I connect my existing account?

Your registration email links your orders automatically. If orders are missing, contact support with your email address.

Can I manage more than one order in one account?

Yes. Multiple orders tied to the same email appear inside one dashboard profile.

If you backed with different emails, support can help merge access once ownership is confirmed.

Community & Forum

Who can post on the forum?

Verified TabletopForge designers only. Forum access in your designer studio.

Community guidelines: read before posting.

Can I share strategy guides or house rules?

Yes. Strategy threads, session reports, solo variants, and house-rule discussions are all encouraged.

We only ask that fan files stay clearly labeled and avoid re-uploading full official assets.

How are spoilers and moderation handled?

Spoiler topics are tagged and moderated. Rules disputes, harassment, or leaked unreleased game details are reviewed by staff moderators.

Repeat violations can lose forum posting access.

Can't find your answer?

Reach out to our support team. Response within 24 hours.
