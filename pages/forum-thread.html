Best solo strategies for Verdant Empires?

Marcus R. 2 hours ago 42 replies 189 views
MR

Marcus R. OP

2 hours ago

I've been playing Verdant Empires solo using the automa rules and I'm struggling to find a consistent strategy. The automa seems to prioritise the eastern wetlands almost every game, which means I end up fighting over the same territory regardless of which faction I play.

Has anyone found a faction that handles the solo pressure particularly well? I'm currently rotating between Ashwood and Tidecroft but neither feels like it has a clear answer to the automa's expansion pattern.

Any strategies, ban-list suggestions, or variant rules that make the solo mode more interesting would be much appreciated.

DO

David Osei Designer

1 hour ago

Great question, Marcus — and you've identified a real automa behaviour. The eastern wetlands priority is intentional: it creates pressure on one predictable axis so you can plan around it, rather than making the automa randomly unpredictable (which our playtesting found more frustrating).

For the solo player specifically: Thornholt is the strongest faction against the wetlands automa because its flood-resistance ability directly counters the automa's primary expansion tool. You won't stop the wetland pressure but you'll suffer much less from it while building elsewhere.

We're also working on a solo campaign variant for Verdant Empires that adds an asymmetric automa AI for each of the six factions — it's being tested now and may ship as a free PnP expansion if backers want it. I'll post a poll in the Publisher Updates thread this week.

SL

Sophie L.

Seconding Thornholt. I've also had good results with a house rule where the automa skips its eastern expansion on any turn where the wetland has more than four of its own tiles. It makes the automa spread more and forces you into multiple defence strategies at once, which I find more interesting.

The PnP expansion that David is teasing sounds incredible. Please make it happen!
