Our Story

Made by gamers. Built for backers.

TabletopForge began as a spreadsheet, a shared obsession, and four friends who refused to wait for someone else to make the games they wanted to play.

97% On-time delivery rate
Founded 2018

We build the games
we couldn't find.

It started in 2018 when four friends decided that the games they wanted to play simply didn't exist yet. We pooled what little we had, rented a studio space, and launched our first Kickstarter with nothing but a spreadsheet and a shared conviction.

What we didn't anticipate: 4,200 backers in the first 48 hours. That campaign — The Iron Pact — shipped on time, under budget, and with a 97% satisfaction rate. We've never looked back.

Today we operate as a fully independent studio. We answer to our backers, not shareholders. Every decision — from component quality to shipping partners — is made with the person waiting at home in mind.

What we stand for

Our principles.

01

Craft over compromise

Every component is held to the same standard we'd demand as players. We'd rather delay a game than ship something that disappoints.

02

Radical transparency

Manufacturing delays, cost overruns, design pivots — our backers hear about it first, always. No spin, no silence.

03

Community as collaborators

Backer feedback has directly shaped every game we've made. Forum suggestions have become official variants. You're not a customer — you're a co-creator.

04

Sustainable by design

We print regionally where possible, use FSC-certified materials, and offset the carbon footprint of every fulfilment run.

05

Accessible to everyone

From print-and-play files to colour-blind-safe component design, we build games that as many people as possible can actually play.

06

Fully independent

No investors, no licence obligations, no brand deals. Our only obligation is to make great games and treat the people who fund them with respect.

Since 2018

Our journey so far.

2018

The Iron Pact launches on Kickstarter

4,200 backers in 48 hours. $312K raised. The four founders quit their day jobs the following Monday.

2019

Studio opens in Bristol, UK

First permanent studio space. First full-time hire. First time we bought a proper coffee machine instead of using the one from someone's flat.

2020

The Iron Pact — Deluxe Edition ships

Despite a global pandemic disrupting our freight routes, every backer received their copy within six weeks of the original estimated window.

2021

Verdant Empires — our biggest campaign yet

5,120 backers. $612K raised. Nominated for three Gen Con awards. The print-and-play alone was downloaded 28,000 times before the physical game shipped.

2023

Backer Portal launches

Real-time shipping tracker, PnP file library, and community forum — all in one place. Built entirely based on backer feedback collected over three years.

2026

Citadels of the Deep — live now

342% funded. 2,847 backers. 14 days left. Our most ambitious game yet — and Moonfall Chronicles is already in development right behind it.
