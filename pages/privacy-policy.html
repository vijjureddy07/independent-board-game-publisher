Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Data Covered Orders, shipping, portal use Only the information needed to fulfil orders, run the platform, and support designers.
What We Do Fulfilment and support only Operational use, service communication, downloads, and legal compliance. No data resale.
Your Rights Access, correction, deletion Users can request updates or removal where retention is not legally required.

1. Introduction

TabletopForge Ltd ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our designer studio or community features.

By using TabletopForge services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Last updated: March 25, 2026

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email address, name — from our platform registration
  • Shipping address — for physical game delivery
  • Order details — amount, edition, game purchased
  • Forum activity — posts, likes, profile data (optional)

2.2 Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, pages visited
  • PnP file downloads (timestamp, file)
  • Forum interactions (views, replies)

2.3 Cookies

We use essential cookies for theme preference, login state. No tracking cookies. Cookie preferences stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Fulfillment: Ship games, send tracking
  • Communication: Game updates, shipping alerts
  • Portal: Track orders, provide PnP downloads
  • Forum: Display posts, enable discussions
  • Analytics: Aggregate stats (never personal)
  • Legal: Comply with tax/shipping regulations

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your data.

  • Shipping: Address to fulfillment partner (deleted after delivery)
  • Crowdfunding: Pledge data from KS/GF APIs
  • Payment: No card details stored (processed by KS/GF)
  • Legal: Only if required by law

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Download data from Dashboard → Settings
  • Rectify: Edit profile/shipping in Dashboard
  • Delete: Contact support (fulfillment records retained 7 years)
  • Object: Unsubscribe from emails (footer link)
  • Portability: Export available on request

EU users: Contact dpo@tabletopforge.com. UK users: UK GDPR applies identically.

6. Security

  • HTTPS everywhere
  • Password hashing (bcrypt)
  • 2FA available (Dashboard → Security)
  • Regular security audits
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant fulfillment partners

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Parents/guardians: contact us.

8. Changes to Policy

Minor changes posted here. Major changes emailed to all registered users. Continued use = acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Contact support or email privacy@tabletopforge.com.
