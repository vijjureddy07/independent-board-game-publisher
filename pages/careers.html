Join the team

Build games. Shape culture.

We're a small, independent studio. Every person here makes a visible difference. We don't hire to fill headcount — we hire when we find someone exceptional.

Team Footprint 8 people · remote-first Small enough for every role to matter, structured enough to ship work cleanly.

Independent, always

No investors, no brand obligations. Decisions are made on craft, not quarterly targets. Your work actually ships.

Small team, big impact

We're eight people. You won't be a cog. You'll be deeply involved across design, production, and community.

Remote-first

Benefits

What we offer.

Competitive salary

Market rate plus a stake in platform performance. The better the platform grows, the better you do.

Free copies of everything

Every TabletopForge title, every edition, delivered to your door. Plus a generous budget for other games.
