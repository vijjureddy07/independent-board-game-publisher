About the company

TabletopForge is an independent board game publisher based in Bristol, UK. Founded by four friends in 2018, we build strategy, campaign, and narrative games distributed exclusively via crowdfunding.

Our games are characterised by asymmetric factions, player-driven emergent narrative, and high component quality. We operate without investors or licensors — our only obligation is to backers.
